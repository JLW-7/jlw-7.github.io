About Me

I’m an 8th grader at YK Pao Middle School in Shanghai. My interests range from competitive swimming to programming to playing violin and volleyball. I enjoy learning new things, which is why I’ve taught myself much of my coding, mathematical modeling, astronomy and even investment (I'm really getting the hang of it, I've earned around $1000 already!).

Sports have shaped a big part of who I am. I’ve dealt with injuries from a broken elbow, to a torn ankle ligament, to much more, but I’ve always worked my way back and kept improving, whether in the pool or on the court. Recovering from setbacks as well as balancing between training and school has made me both disciplined and resilient.

I'm also passionate about my community and really enjoy making an impact. I've taken on many leadership roles in school, founded clubs that encourage learning, and given motivational speeches in front of the whole school about my experiences. I love using tech to assist people as well. Some of the programming projects I’ve created are used by classmates and teachers, which motivates me to keep building things that help others.

And for the record, I am a girl who just happens to like short hair and the freedom of not fitting into anyone’s stereotype.
